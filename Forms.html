Ban forms

Submit to appeal your ban. It could take 1-2 days for us to review your appeal. While your appeal is being reviewed, do not remind staff to check your appeal. To check the status of your ban appeal, or to read other players' appeals, If your appeal is initially denied, you can re-appeal, but please wait 7 days before posting a new appeal.

Why do we give appealable bans instead of tempbans?
There are a few reasons to this. First, we want to see that you actually are interested in returning. Only people who actually are interested in returning will spend the time required to write an appeal. Second, we want to make sure that the players who do return understand what they did wrong. The questions we ask help us assess your understanding of this. Finally, in the case of some bans, we need to prepare for you to return.

Trainee applications

Submit to apply for the trainee position. While your application is being reviewed, do not remind staff to check your application. When you log in, you will be automatically promoted if your application has been accepted. If your application was not initially accepted, we still may accept it in the future.
